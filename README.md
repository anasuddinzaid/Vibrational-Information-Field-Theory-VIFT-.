> **Reality emerges from a universal field of information expressed as structured vibrations, governed by stability-selection biases that give rise to what we interpret as the laws of physics.**

The theory attempts to answer a deeper class of questions such as:

* *Why does physics have laws at all instead of chaos?*
* *Why do stable particles exist instead of instant quantum dispersion?*
* *Why is gravity attractive and not neutral or repulsive by default?*
* *Why does consciousness arise in highly optimized biological matter?*
* *Why does the universe expand in a scale-invariant and structured manner?*
* *Why is light possible in this universe but not guaranteed in all universes?*

The framework argues that:

* The universe is a **surviving low-loss resonance branch** from an infinite space of possible vibrational information configurations.

* Matter persists because it is a **low-entropy, high-coherence vibration mode** in the information field.
* Energy is the **amplitude of information excitation**, not a separate entity.
* Gravity is a **macroscopic gradient of information-density bias** that curves spacetime as an emergent effect.
* Consciousness arises when a system enters a **self-stabilizing resonance loop that locally minimizes entropy**.
* Cosmogenesis is a **vibrational burst triggered when information collapses into a high-density seed**.

This theory is intentionally kept **math-free here**, focusing only on conceptual clarity and theoretical architecture. Mathematical formalization is part of a long-term plan after the age of 25.
